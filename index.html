About

I am a senior undergraduate majoring in computer science at the School of Information Engineering, China University of Geosciences (Beijing)—CUGB.

I will obtain my Bachelor of Engineering degree in 2025. My experiences include working at GISense Lab at The University of Texas at Austin, ZJU_Earth_Data Lab at Zhejiang University and serving as a research assistant with Dr. Shan Ye.

My primary research interests are centered on AI and Data Science, with a special focus on Human-centered Geospatial Data Science, GeoAI, disaster prevention, and ecology. These interests are aimed at enhancing human welfare. Additionally, my work extends to AI4Science, particularly in Earth-related disciplines such as geochemistry and paleontology.
