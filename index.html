About

I’m a first-year Master’s student in the Department of Geography and Sustainability at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science in 2025 from the School of Information Engineering at China University of Geosciences (Beijing) — CUGB.

My research lies on GeoAI, with a particular emphasis on disaster prevention, Human-centered Geospatial Data Science, and ecology, all aimed at enhancing human welfare. I also applied AI techniques to Earth-related disciplines such as geochemistry and paleontology.
