About

I am a senior undergraduate student major in computer science in the School of Information Engineering at the China University of Geosciences (Beijing)-CUGB.

My primary research interest lies in employing data-driven methods and Artificial Intelligence to explore and address Earth-related problems across various disciplines, including geography, atmospheric science, oceanography, geochemistry, geophysics, paleobiology and more.

I am engaged in data-driven paleobiology research under the supervision of Dr. Shan Ye. Simultaneously, I contribute as a back-end developer to the High Temperature Geochemistry Benchmark Database. Additionally, I actively participate in Rock Geochemistry research, applying network analysis methodologies, under the supervision of Dr. Zhangzhou J. .
